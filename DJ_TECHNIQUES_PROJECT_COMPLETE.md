## 🎧 How to Listen & Verify

### Phase 1: Early Transitions (7-8 seconds before outro)

1. Open rendered audio in player or editor
2. Find last 45 seconds of a track (outro section)
3. Listen **7-8 seconds BEFORE the outro officially ends**
4. You'll hear the next track quietly fading in
5. Both tracks play together for ~16 bars
6. **Result:** Smooth, professional blend (not abrupt)

### Phase 2: Bass Control (at transition point)

1. Go to exact transition point (where tracks meet)
2. Focus on LOW frequencies (kick drum, bass)
3. Use headphones with good bass response
4. Listen for clean bass entry (no muddy "cloud")
5. Compare to non-enhanced version if possible
6. **Result:** Clean bass, tight blending (no mud)

### Phase 4: Dynamic Variation (across mix)

1. Play entire mix
2. Listen to transition 1 (smooth or snappy?)
3. Listen to transition 2 (different strategy?)
4. Listen to transition 3 (varied again?)
5. Notice patterns aren't repetitive/mechanical
6. **Result:** Natural DJ mixing (not robotic)
